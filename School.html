St Francis of Assisi

"Supporting every student to grow in faith and learning."

St Francis of Assisi is located on 1 Stone St, Glendenning NSW. It is part of the Catholic Schools Parramatta Diocese's system whcich is made up of 80 Catholic primary and secondary schools across NSW with more than 43,500 students . St Francis of Assisi is a vibrant and contemporary school offering quality education for students. The dedicated teachers are highly skilled and passionate about providing the best learning opportunities for every student.The majority of Catholic Schools Parramatta Diocese teachers have more than five years of teaching experience and hold two or more professional qualifications. St Francis of Assisi also provides students with the top most quality of play facilities from play equipment to learning resources.
